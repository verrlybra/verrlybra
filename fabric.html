The Fabric Story

Textile engineering focused on disappearing comfort.

Woven, not molded.

At Verrly, we believe structure should adapt to skin texture perfectly. Instead of injection-molded thick synthetic fillers, our pieces rely entirely on custom stretch recovery fibers woven directly inside our base fabrics.

French-Loom Silk Blends

Our signature lace wraps adapt organically to accurate body movements without digging or leaving harsh lines. Woven precisely to breathe and shift heat away naturally all day.

Second-Skin Microfiber

A moisture-wicking weave composed of ultra-fine flat filaments that block visibility lines completely, making it the perfect layer underneath sheer tailored tops.

01

Zero Friction Under-Bands

By shifting to advanced bonded micro-tapes instead of traditional thick, exposed elastic borders, Verrly underwear balances weight completely across the ribs.

02

Breathable by Design

An open-weave lace structure allows continuous airflow, keeping the fabric cool against skin even through long, warm days.

03

Small-Batch Milling

Every roll of fabric is milled in limited runs, reducing offcut waste and keeping our supply chain accountable end to end.

04

Recovery-Tested Stretch

Each blend is cycle-tested to hold its shape through hundreds of wears and washes without losing structural recovery.
