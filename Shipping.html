02 Returns

We want you to love what you ordered. If something isn't right, you may return unworn, unwashed items in their original packaging within 30 days of delivery for a full refund to your original payment method.

  • Items must have original tags and hygiene liner (where applicable) intact
  • Sale and clearance items are final sale unless faulty
  • Refunds are issued within 5–7 business days of us receiving your return

How to start a return

Email hello@verrly.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send a prepaid return label and instructions.

03 Exchanges

Need a different size or colorway? We're happy to exchange any unworn item within 30 days. The fastest way is to place a new order for the size you need and return the original for a refund — this avoids any wait time while we process the swap.

04 Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged, faulty, or isn't what you ordered, contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the item. We'll cover return shipping and send a replacement or full refund — no questions asked.
